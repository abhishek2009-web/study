Green strips = strontium aluminate coating (edge lines, lane dividers, zebra crossing).

By day the strips sit dormant while charging silently from UV. From dusk they discharge as visible light — brightest right after sunset, fading gradually until the next charge cycle. Watch the meters track the exact mechanism described in the build document.
